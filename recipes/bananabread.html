Ingrdients

  • Flour - 1 1/2 cup
  • Oil - 1 cup
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Mashed Banana - 1 1/2 cups
  • Eggs - 2
  • Vanilla Extract - 1 tsp
  • Baking Powder - 1 tsp
  • Bicarbonate of Soda - 1 tsp
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp
  1. Mix oil, sugar, vanilla extract and eggs in a bowl
  2. Mix the rest of the ingredients in a separate bowl
  3. Combine both mixtures and stir thoroughly until no dry flour is visible
  4. Oil a baking tin and pour mixture up to half full (allow for rising)
  5. Preheat Oven to 160ÂºC and bake for 45-60 min or until golden brown
